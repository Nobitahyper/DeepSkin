Advanced Skin Lesion Segmentation with Deep Learning

Disclaimer: This tool is for educational purposes only. Please consult with a healthcare professional for a definitive diagnosis.

Skin cancer is one of the most common cancers globally. Early detection can play a pivotal role in successful treatment. This automated tool harnesses state-of-the-art deep learning models to perform skin lesion segmentation, aiding in the early detection of potential skin cancer regions. Early identification and treatment can significantly enhance positive outcomes.

How to use:
1. Upload a clear skin image.
2. Choose the desired model for analysis.
3. Click on 'Predict' to get the results.

Upload a skin image to detect potential cancerous regions.
